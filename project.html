Indeed, it was a tight race in many of the major categories where no apparent frontrunners seemed to emerge. Going into the night, we didn’t know who would walk away with the golden statuettes, be we knew to expect a night of high glamour from nominees. Anora star Mikey Madison has become this season’s fashion darling, while Demi Moore has used fashion to nod to her character in The Substance. Wicked co-stars Cynthia Erivo and Ariana Grande have also produced high-fashion homages to their characters in everything from prize vintage to custom couture. The men have also represented on the fashion front, with the ever-stylish Colman Domingo and the singular Jeremy Strong among the nominees.
